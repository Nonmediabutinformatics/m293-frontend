How It Works

Once you log in, simply type in your task in the input box labeled "Task", and click "Add Task" to add it to your to-do list. You can mark a task as "Done" by clicking the "Done" button next to its name, and it will be moved to the "Done" list. If you change your mind, you can always click "Undo" to move it back to the "To Do" list. To delete a task, simply click the "Delete" button next to its name. You can also edit the name of a task by double-clicking on its name and pressing enter after making your changes.
